The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Batman as he faces off against his greatest enemy, the Joker, who wreaks havoc on Gotham City.

Key Information

  • Release Year: 2008
  • Director: Christopher Nolan
  • Genre: Action, Crime, Drama
  • Runtime: 152 minutes

Cast & Creative Team

  • Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman
  • Heath Ledger as The Joker
  • Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face
  • Gary Oldman as James Gordon
  • Morgan Freeman as Lucius Fox

Main Characters

Batman, The Joker, Harvey Dent, James Gordon, and Lucius Fox play major roles in the film's intense storyline.

Reviews

The Dark Knight received widespread critical acclaim, with praise for Heath Ledger's performance as the Joker and the film's gripping narrative.
