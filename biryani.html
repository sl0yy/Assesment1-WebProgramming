Delicious Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish layered with spiced meat and aromatic basmati rice. It's a favorite dish in South Asian cuisine, perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or lamb, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk

Method

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then cook until 70% done.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee/oil and fry the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices, and cook until aromatic.
  4. Add the meat and yogurt, cook until the meat is tender.
  5. Layer the cooked rice over the meat mixture and drizzle saffron milk on top.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat (dum method) for 20 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves. Serve hot!
